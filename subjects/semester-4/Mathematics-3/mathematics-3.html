Books

Discrete Mathematics and its Applications

by Kenneth H. Rosen, Tata McGraw – Hill

Description

Discrete Mathematics And Its Applications (SIE) is a study of mathematical structures that are fundamentally discrete rather than continuous. Discrete objects can often be enumerated by integers, and more formally, deal with countable sets. It helps demonstrate the relevance and practicality of Discrete Mathematics to a wide variety of real-world applications.
